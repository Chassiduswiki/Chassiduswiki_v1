## Definitions

the Middos are called the children of [[Chochmah - חכמה]] and [[Binah - בינה]], or, alternatively, [[Tiferes - תפארת]] (or the first six Middos in unison) is called the "son" and [[Malchus - מלכות]] is called the "daughter"
